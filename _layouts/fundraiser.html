Fundraiser

You can also support us by purchasing prints made for and during the launch of sounds about riso .online:
-James Bascara’s print in Cornflower/Gold or Federal Blue/Orange.
-Natalie Andrewson’s Coast to Coast print made during a live draw & print with Zine Hug on risoTV.
-Lucky risograph’s Color Separation Anxiety print made during a live file separation workshop on risoTV.
A portion of sales will go to sounds about riso and the other will go directly to the artist.
All funds raised will be used for expenses used to bring the gallery and events online. Any additional money raised will be used to keep soundsaboutriso.online running and to continuously curate more events on risoTV.
