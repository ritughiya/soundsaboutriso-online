“sounds about riso x abC Art Book Fair 2021 ➦
Keep in Touch Riso Collab Project will be highlighted in the Material and Printing section.
July 9 - July 11, 2021
"Keep in Touch" Riso collab project is curated by sounds about riso and abC art book fair. From March to July 2021, 12 Riso studios have participated in this project. They have located on both sides of the Pacific Ocean: Beijing, Shanghai, Shenzhen, Ningbo, Taichung, New York, Grand Rapids, Los Angeles. Two studios formed a creative team, one from China and the other from the United States. They communicated with each other through email and Zoom call, providing a local proverb/idiom for each other as a basis of creation, and started Risograph printing.
